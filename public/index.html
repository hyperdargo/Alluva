How Torrent Streaming Works

This app uses TorrServer or WebTorrent to stream torrents directly without downloading. When you click play, the torrent is fetched from indexers, streamed in chunks, and played in your chosen player (VLC/MPV/browser). The VLC extension routes streams to your local VLC for smooth playback of any format.

About Alluva

Alluva was originally built as Stream Vault — a personal media hub for streaming anime, movies, and TV shows. The goal was simple: one place to search, browse, and play everything.

Over time it grew — better filtering, torrent streaming, VLC/MPV integration, a suggestion system, per-section filters, and a full admin workflow. The UI got redesigned with per-view accent colors and iOS-style navigation.

The name Alluva came later — "all of it" — because that's what it is. Movies, TV, anime, direct play, torrents, all of it in one place. One word. Rolls off the tongue.
